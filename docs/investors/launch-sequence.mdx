<H2 id="wave-1">Launch + one week · exit strategies</H2>
<Para>
  Dale gains a structured exit layer. Take profit ladders. Trailing stops. Hard floors.
  DCA out over a defined window. The patterns are not new in trading, they are new for a
  bot that can apply them in plain language to your specific position.
</Para>

<H2 id="wave-2">Launch + two weeks · prediction markets</H2>
<Para>
  Polymarket and Kalshi through AGG.market. Best price routing across venues. Same wallet,
  same PIN, same conversation. Dale trades tokens and events from one place. The unified
  wallet thesis extends past spot.
</Para>

<H2 id="wave-3">Launch + three weeks · leagues</H2>
<Para>
  Five ranks go live. XP starts accruing across every action. Sharp Shooter unlocks 25%
  referral commission. Whale Hunter unlocks early access to new chains. Legend unlocks
  custom Dale personality. The community layer begins to feel like a place.
</Para>

<H2 id="wave-4">Launch + four weeks · streaks, badges, competitions</H2>
<Para>
  Twelve plus badge types. Streak tracking with milestone celebrations. The Sprint, the
  Sniper, Volume Wars, Diamond Hands. The Crown becomes daily. The leaderboard becomes
  queryable through Dale.
</Para>

<H2 id="month-2">Launch + two months · full social layer</H2>
<Para>
  Win cards generated automatically on profitable trades. Welcome cards minted on signup.
  Following sharp traders. Copy trading with rule based execution. Paper trading with
  cross leaderboard visibility. Social messaging through Dale, never as direct DMs. Three
  tier opt-in. Surprise gifts from the team.
</Para>

<Pull>
  At month two, every winning trade becomes marketing material. The community grows on user wins.
</Pull>

<H2 id="month-3">Launch + three months · $BOBBY token, ecosystem rewards</H2>
<Para>
  $BOBBY ships as Bobby's own token, layered into the existing platform economy. Ecosystem
  rewards unlock for holders across both products. The two engine, one pool architecture
  gets a second token without changing the unified pool thesis.
</Para>

<H2 id="ongoing">Ongoing · what gets added when audited</H2>
<Para>
  New chains roll out as audits clear. Privacy swaps through RocketX. Eterna for perps.
  Voice messages in. Voice PIN. Dale voice replies. Bobby Trending Channel as a curated
  signal funnel. The Dale website widget for non Telegram users. The Mini App for unified
  wallet UI. Each one is an announcement when it ships, not a promise on this page.
</Para>

<Callout kind="info" title="How this page updates">
  When a wave ships, the section above moves into present tense and a new line appears

</Callout>

<H2 id="why">Why phased</H2>
<Para>
  Three reasons. <Lime>Each wave is its own announcement</Lime>, which means
  the platform stays in the conversation for months instead of one news cycle. Each wave
  gets pressure tested before the next one lands, which means quality holds. And the
  community sees the platform getting stronger every week, which is the kind of momentum
  you cannot buy.
</Para>
