<Lede>The plain English version.</Lede>

<Para>Crypto is digital assets. Coins like Bitcoin and Ethereum, plus thousands of smaller ones traded on exchanges and through specialized tools.</Para>

<Para>A Telegram trading bot is a chat program inside Telegram that lets you trade by messaging it. You don't leave the app you already use.</Para>

<Para>DeepBot is a platform of four products that share intelligence. Dale handles your trades when you talk to him. Bobby watches crypto Telegram groups at scale and surfaces what's moving. Wallet holds your keys safely. DeepBot Link is the web interface, coming soon.</Para>

<Para>Built for people who want to trade crypto without learning a complicated dashboard. Type what you want. The platform handles the rest.</Para>
