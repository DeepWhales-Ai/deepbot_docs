<H2 id="frame">The frame</H2>
<Para>
  Most crypto tools are single source. A bot watches a chain. A scanner reads a contract. A
  signal feed parses X. Each is one input, one perspective, one easy thing to game.
</Para>
<Para>
  DeepBot is built on the opposite premise. The platform's three products operate at three
  different layers of the market, and they share what each detects. <Lime>Bobby
  detects from inside thousands of communities. Dale executes from inside the user's
  conversation. Wallet holds the keys for both.</Lime> Each layer's signal sharpens the next.
</Para>

<H2 id="bobby-side">The Bobby side</H2>
<Para>
  Bobby lives in project Telegram groups. It runs the chat surfaces, the trending lists, the
  ad rails, and the project side intelligence loop. It sees coordinated activity across
  communities, in real time, at a scale single channel tools cannot reach.
</Para>

<H2 id="dale-side">The Dale side</H2>
<Para>
  Dale is the trader facing surface. It uses what the platform detects to inform every quote,
  every scan, every refusal. When a coordinated push fires across communities, Dale sees it
  before retail does and surfaces it in plain language to the user.
</Para>

<H2 id="wallet-side">The Wallet side</H2>
<Para>
  Wallet is the visual control panel. Telegram native, fully self custody, multi chain. It
  holds the same private keys Dale signs with, presented through a Phantom style GUI for the
  moments when you want to look instead of type. <Lime>One wallet. Two interfaces.
  Pick the one you want today.</Lime>
</Para>
<Para>
  The wallet panel inherits the safety floor from Dale. Same PIN gate. Same auto delete on
  sensitive messages. Same encrypted key isolation. Open <Mono>@walletdeepbot</Mono> directly
  or type <Mono>open wallet</Mono> inside Dale. Same wallet, same balance, same history. Just
  a different door.
</Para>

<H2 id="encoding">The encoding moat</H2>
<Pull>
  The intelligence cannot be extracted without trading through Dale. This is the moat, and
  it is structural.
</Pull>
<Para>
  Every Bobby alert is encoded for Dale's tool call schema. Not raw contract addresses you
  can paste anywhere. The intelligence is structured so that only Dale can interpret and
  execute on it. Other bots see something that looks like noise. Dale sees the trade.
</Para>
<Para>
  If you want the intelligence, you trade through Dale. There is no leakage path. There is
  no <Mono>subscribe to alerts and execute elsewhere</Mono>. Bobby and Dale are
  bonded at the data layer in a way the rest of the market cannot replicate without
  rebuilding five years of distribution from scratch.
</Para>
<Para>
  Every Bobby signal funnels to Dale. Every Dale trade generates fees.
  Every fee feeds the unified pool that every $DEEPAI holder shares.{' '}
  <Lime>The flywheel is closed by design.</Lime>
</Para>

<H2 id="rule">The hard rule</H2>
<Callout kind="info" title="Bobby and Dale never publicly cross paths">
  In a project group, Bobby will not advertise Dale. In Dale's conversations, Dale will not
  reveal the Bobby network as the source of a signal. The brand layer keeps them separate.
  The platform layer connects them. This document is the only place the connection is stated
  plainly.
</Callout>
<Para>
  Wallet sits adjacent to that rule rather than under it. It is the key custody layer for
  both products. Users who arrive through Dale meet Wallet as <Mono>open wallet</Mono>.
  Users who arrive through <Mono>@walletdeepbot</Mono> never need to meet Dale to use it.
  The platform meets each user where they are.
</Para>
