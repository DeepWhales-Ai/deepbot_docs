<H2 id="new-pairs">New Pairs</H2>
<Para>
  The Ethereum new pair detector. Catches every token at the moment liquidity
  is added on chain. Filters through Blockaid safety scanning, honeypot
  detection, and contract risk checks before any alert publishes.
</Para>
<Para>
  Available to PRO subscribers. Powers <Lime>Shotgun Blue</Lime>.
</Para>

<H2 id="trend">Trend</H2>
<Para>
  The cross channel trending detector. Surfaces tokens with sentiment weighted,
  multi source activity. Sentiment classification, risk level, Blockaid
  scoring, market data, and quick links on every alert.
</Para>
<Para>
  Available to PRO subscribers. Powers <Lime>Shotgun Trend</Lime>.
</Para>

<H2 id="signal-wins">Signal Wins</H2>
<Para>
  The public wins feed. When alerts from the New Pairs or Trend feeds hit
  milestone returns, they get posted here.
</Para>
<Para>
  This is the celebratory channel. Bundle detection at launch, Blockaid
  safety scoring, automatic honeypot handling, milestone tracking from
  effective entry price, mega celebrations on 1000x and 2000x.
</Para>
<Para>
  Anyone can join. Find it at <Mono>@deepbotwins</Mono>.
</Para>

<Callout kind="lime" title="Signals private, wins public">

</Callout>
