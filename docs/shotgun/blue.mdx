<H2 id="feed">The feed</H2>
<Para>
  The New Pairs feed surfaces tokens at the moment liquidity is first added on
  chain. The platform watches every factory contract on Ethereum and catches
  each new pool as it lands.
</Para>
<Para>
  Most new pairs go to zero in hours. That is why the feed runs through safety
  scoring before anything reaches Shotgun.
</Para>

<H2 id="score">The prediction score</H2>
<Para>
  Every alert in the Blue feed carries a prediction score. Format:
</Para>

<div className="db-quote">
  <Mono>63 green / 37 red</Mono>
</div>

<Para>
  The score is built from historical pattern matching against years of new
  pair outcomes. <Lime>Green weighting</Lime> means the launch matches
  patterns of tokens that survived. <Lime>Red weighting</Lime> flags patterns
  associated with rugs, traps, and immediate fails.
</Para>
<Para>
  Higher green is not a guarantee. It is a probabilistic edge based on how
  this launch shape has performed in the past.
</Para>
