<Para>
  Compromised keys. Malicious contracts. Slippage attacks. A second of inattention that ends
  with an empty wallet. These are the things that have actually rugged people you know. We
  designed the platform around the assumption that every one of them is happening to someone
  right now.
</Para>
<Para>
  Your wallet keys live behind an encryption layer the trading code cannot read. Even when

  three required, stored as hashes the platform cannot reverse.

<Para>

</Para>

<H2 id="forgot">What happens when you forget your PIN</H2>
<Para>
  You say <Mono>I need a PIN reset</Mono>. Dale walks you through the three
  secret questions you chose yourself at PIN setup. The questions are personal, the answers
  were yours, and the platform stores only hashes it cannot reverse. All three questions,
  all three correct. No partial credit. A correct full set unlocks a PIN reset path. Dale
  asks you to set a new PIN. The new PIN takes effect immediately. The wallet stays locked
  the entire time, so even during reset, no transaction can move.
</Para>
<Para>
  If you cannot answer the secret questions, the team handles it. Dale flags the case to the
  admin channel with a verification request. The team checks identity against on chain
  history, account context, and wallet activity that only the real account holder would
  know. Once verified, the team resets the PIN and you are notified in the same chat. Your
  wallet stays locked until then. Funds are safe. Nothing moves.
</Para>

<Callout kind="default" title="Friction ordering is the security model">
  Every recovery path has a cost in time, deliberately. The self serve path takes a minute.
  The human review path takes longer. Easy for the real user, expensive for an attacker,
  impossible for an attacker without your three answers and the team's verification.
</Callout>
