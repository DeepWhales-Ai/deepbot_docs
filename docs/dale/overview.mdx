<H2 id="is">What Dale is</H2>
<Para>
  Dale is the trader facing product of DeepBot. You ask Dale to swap. Dale checks the route,
  surfaces fees, executes. You ask Dale to watch a launch. Dale watches and pings you. You
  ask Dale to protect your bag from a rug. Dale watches the mempool and exits before the
  attacker can.
</Para>

<H2 id="is-not">What Dale is not</H2>
<Para>
  Dale is not an assistant that explains crypto to you. It is not a research tool with a
  chat box on the side. It is not a generic LLM with a wallet glued on. Dale has tool access,
  deterministic execution, persistent memory, and a defined voice. The model is the brain.
  The tools are the hands. The voice is the brand.
</Para>

<H2 id="feels">What Dale feels like</H2>
<Pull>
  It feels like a trading partner who never gets tired, never sleeps, never misses, and
  never makes a typo.
</Pull>
