<H2 id="what">What it is</H2>
<Para>
  The <Mono>/trade</Mono> panel is the visual control surface for the same
  routing layer Dale uses in conversation. Open it with <Mono>/trade</Mono>{' '}
  inside Dale. Send, swap, create wallets, review balances, and access
  strategies without typing the request.
</Para>

<H2 id="pin">PIN parity</H2>
<Para>
  Every send from the panel runs through the same PIN gate as Dale in
  conversation. The threshold you set in chat applies in the panel. The PIN
  unlocks the same session. There is no separate panel only credential, and
  there is no panel only bypass.
</Para>

<H2 id="strategies">Strategies and Auto Buy</H2>
<Para>
  Strategies and Auto Buy are surfaced higher in the panel menu. Take profit
  ladders, trailing stops, hard floors, and conditional entries are accessible
  in one step from the top level. The placement matches how often the
  controls are used.
</Para>

<H2 id="feed">The new pairs feed</H2>
<Para>
  The pairs feed in the panel shows fresh launches across the chains you have
  enabled. Tap any row to scan, then trade or skip. The feed is live across
  EVM and Solana.
</Para>
