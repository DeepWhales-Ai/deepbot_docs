<H2 id="create">Create or import</H2>
<Para>
  Dale provisions wallets automatically on first message. To import an existing wallet, send
  Dale the private key in a private message. It is processed in memory, encrypted, and the
  original message auto deletes from chat.
</Para>

<H2 id="default">Default wallet for trading</H2>
<Para>
  Dale picks the chain by your stated intent and your funds. Set a default chain in settings
  if you trade one chain ninety percent of the time.
</Para>

<H2 id="transfer">Transfer assets</H2>
<Para>
  Plain language. Address, ENS, contact name, or Telegram handle. Dale quotes gas, runs
  recipient safety checks, and asks to confirm before signing. Sample dialogue at the top
  of this page.
</Para>

<H2 id="rename">Rename and delete</H2>
<Para>
  <Mono>rename wallet 2 to "trading"</Mono>. <Mono>delete wallet 4</Mono>.
  Plain language. Dale confirms before destroying any keys.
</Para>
